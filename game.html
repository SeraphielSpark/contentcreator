OLYMPIAN GAME

Take on the role of Zeus, King of the Gods, and battle through 30 levels to restore peace to the mortal realm and Olympus.

The Rise of Zeus

In the beginning, the world was ruled by the Titans, ancient and powerful beings who held dominion over the cosmos.

But Cronus, the Titan King, feared a prophecy that one of his children would overthrow him. So he devoured each of them at birth.

All except one - Zeus. Hidden away on the island of Crete, he grew in power and wisdom.

Now grown, Zeus has returned to challenge the Titans and free his siblings from their eternal prison within Cronus.

The fate of Olympus rests in your hands. Wield the power of lightning and thunder to restore balance to the world!
